All you have to do is follow your nose to The Stinking Rose, one of San Francisco’s most unique and entertaining dining experiences. Located in the heart of North Beach, San Francisco’s renowned Little Italy, the garlic restaurant has become famous for celebrating the euphoria of garlic. Offering scrumptious, contemporary, California-Italian cuisine prepared and adorned with garlic, there is hearty fare for the truly adventurous, mild for the novice and sans garlic for vampires.
